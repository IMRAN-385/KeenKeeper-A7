# 🌿 KeenKeeper

> Keep your friendships alive — your personal shelf of meaningful connections.

KeenKeeper is a relationship management app that helps you stay in touch with the people who matter most. Browse, tend, and nurture your friendships with smart tracking and reminders.

## ✨ Key Features

### 1. 👫 Friend Tracking
See all your friends at a glance with their contact status — **On-Track**, **Almost Due**, or **Overdue** — so you never lose touch with the people who matter.

### 2. ⚡ Quick Check-In
Log a **Call**, **Text**, or **Video** interaction directly from a friend's detail page. Every interaction is automatically saved to your personal Timeline.

### 3. 📊 Friendship Analytics
Visual **donut chart** showing your interaction history broken down by type — see whether you call, text, or video chat your friends the most.
